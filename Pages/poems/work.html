Trigger Warnings // Depression & Suicidal Thoughts

What way to start they can't find a beat. My heart is gone.

The empty carcasses of bones lost in memories and deep hidden zones. I use the tones that carry weight in my voice. They make me stronger and tougher.
Bigger and buffer.
I try not to suffer.

I bring myself pain that drives me too rain down my names. I forgive you I just need something inside my brain. Something to make this time feel worth it.

Yet I need a permit to start building.
Just a home.
Somewhere that's so soft to touch and so rough it can bare all you're problems. I am talking to the wallpaper that stops me like a jawbreaker the last time you were my screen saver. I am innovative with my self-harm but that's not what my teachers meant when they called me an innovator.

I speak so much nonsense you'll need a translator. I've committed a crime and the police have there best illustrator. I need an educator to teach me something other than pain. I need someone to tell me that getting high isn't the worst way to end the pain. I need something to bring me to back away from the edge but all I see are veins. I am doing 90 on I-90 and I am constantily switching lanes.

You call it a distraction I call it a coping skill. I call it a coping skill you call it poisin. I say I want to die you say I don't.
You refues to understand me I say I'll go.

I am trapped in my thoughs when I try so hard to get out. I slam on the walls beging to to be let free. I walk slowly to the end of the sea.
Ya see?
You know me?
You don't know me.
