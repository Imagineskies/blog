Photography

I've loved photography with a passion since I was in middle school. The beauty of capturing a thousand words in breath taking images is what I live for. So when I finally got the chance to use a DSLR, I was ecstatic. The first camera I ever used was a Cannon Rebel T6i which I borrowed from my school. It helped me get familiar with learning the basics of photography as I tinkered with the different modes that were available. After 9th grade I got my first camera, a Nikon D70. Now that I had my own camera I was able to spend hours outside of school taking pictures. From Detroit, Michigan to Boston, Massachusetts, I've learned a lot over the course of 4 years. This is my passion, one I'll always hold close to home.
